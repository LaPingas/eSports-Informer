What is "eSports"?

eSport, similar to regular sport, is a form of competition using video games.
Since video games exist, there was always the demand for multi-player games in which 2 people or more can play the same game together and experience the same events at the same time.
While multi-player games can make for a fun and casual time with your friends, eSports take it to a different level - in the way of competition.
Also similar to regular sports is the fact that there are a lot of eSports titles in different genres, which makes for a lot of variety - you will always find some sort of game you can have fun with (like sports!).
eSports started as a small thing, but in the modern times eSport tournaments can gather a lot of attention, competitors and prize-money, which bring teams and sponsers to step in and level up the competition.
Hopefully now you understand what does "eSport" means, so you can feel free to browse the site while understanding the concept of eSports.
